# Constants
## Positive color
 - #6CE0D1
## Negative color
 - #E06C89
## Interaction and hover animate duration
 - 500 miliseconds
## Interaction and hover brightness value multiply factor
 - 0.7
# Description
 - Positive and negative buttons are buttons that indicate confirmation, rejection, exiting, or other similar functions
    - Exiting and rejections are negative
    - Confirmation and accepting are positive
 - Positive buttons should have [this color](##positive-color)
 - Negative buttons should have [this color](##negative-color)
 - They should all have white text/icon colors
 - They should all have the same hover and interaction animation
    - Their brightness values should be multipied by a [this factor](##interaction-and-hover-brightness-value-multiply-factor) to dim them when hovered or interacted with
    - Hover and interaction animation will play over [this amount of time](##interaction-and-hover-animate-duration)
